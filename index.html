Bitpaint

Bitpaint is a simple painting app for color studies, plein air, and sketches.


Become a beta tester

Highlights:

  • Customize basic tools with modes to alter texture, shape, and blending behavior
  • Adjust the color, width, and position of a stroke after it's on the canvas
  • Reduce screen clutter by showing only the buttons you want to see
  • Export directly to your favorite image-sharing apps

Plus the features you're used to:

  • Undo and redo
  • Gesture-based zoom and pan
  • Eyedropper
  • Crop
  • HSV or RGB color pickers
